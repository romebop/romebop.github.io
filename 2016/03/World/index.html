Hello, World!

Mar 31, 2016

I had a lot of fun setting up a personal site for the first time and learning about how static site generators work. I decided to go with Hexo which runs on top of Node.js. It seems to be among the more popular ones with lots of community driven content and plugins to play with. Hosting was conveniently available through GitHub Pages, and things came together quite simply, which was nice.

Moving Forward

I don’t have much blogging experience aside from keeping a Tumblr, which mainly just ended up being a collection of random media I’ve come across and found interesting. I hope this blog can be a collection of more processed ideas about my interests, whether it’s about music, technology, culture, or something else.

A big motivation for me to want to create this space has been the idea of documenting my progress as I continue to grow as a person and programmer. Being able to reflect on my thoughts and express them coherently seems useful, and I like the idea of being able to look back to see how far I’ve come, and then look forward to place I’d like to reach. Hopefully the content may be useful to some people in the future as well.

I think this will be fun.

Let’s get it.
